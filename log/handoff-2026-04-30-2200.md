## Anxiety Filter (Lessons for Future Coco y Mango Stories)

Hard rules learned this session — apply in any future story:
- NO death of grandparents/loved ones, even spiritualized.
- NO seasonal alignment to Día de Muertos / Halloween in stories that imply loss (the season cue itself triggers anxiety).
- NO beloved recurring characters (Don Nacho, la maestra, los compañeritos) in real danger / trapped / hurt.
- NO natural disasters with consequences (earthquakes, fires, floods) — the disaster itself is the trigger, not the resolution.
- Predator scares OK only if (a) brief, (b) resolved within seconds, (c) the predator never makes contact, (d) the scare is followed by clear safety + wonder.
- Drama YES (stage fright, lost ticket, getting lost briefly, mild physical comedy). Fear NO (death, abandonment, real injury, structural disaster).
